Top three places to visit in Stuttgart

Mercedes-Benz Museum

Explore iconic cars and the rich history of the Mercedes-Benz brand.

Schlossplatz

The central square of Stuttgart, perfect for relaxing and city walks.

Wilhelma Zoo & Botanical Garden

A unique zoo and botanical garden with animals and plants from around the world.

Food to try

Maultaschen

German dumplings often compared to ravioli.

Spätzle

Soft egg noodles, often served with cheese (Käsespätzle).

Apfelküchle

Fried apple rings - a perfect Swabian dessert.
